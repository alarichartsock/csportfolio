Chapters 1-10

The book starts with explaining how to write out numbers in base 8, 4, and 2 and how to convert between base 10, 8, 4 and 2 and add/subtract/multiply/divide numbers from base 10,8,4,2 into 10,8,4,2. It also goes into how relays were invented (basically the basis of computing, their design relates to transistors heavily). And then they go into logic gates and later explain how they can be built together to create more complex circuits. So it helps you understand computers on a basic level.
